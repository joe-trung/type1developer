What is T1D?

This is where Type 1 Diabetes meet Software Developer.

Type 1 Diabetes

Type 1 diabetes is a chronic autoimmune condition where the pancreas produces little to no insulin. It requires lifelong insulin therapy to manage blood sugar levels. It is typically diagnosed in childhood and requires careful monitoring and medical management. There is no cure and no prevention yet.

About

Timeline of notable events.

  • 05/02/2022

    Starting Point

    I experienced a significant event in my life when I was hospitalized and received a diagnosis of type 1 diabetes. Transitioning from a previously healthy individual to a patient with a chronic disease was a shocking experience. However, I swiftly adapted and embraced the new reality, determined to regain control over my life. I embarked on a journey of learning how to effectively manage my condition, empowering myself to stay on top of the challenges that come with it.

  • November 2022

    Turning Point

    Six months after my diagnosis, I came to the realization that my physical strength had diminished. However, I discovered that my mental fortitude had grown stronger than ever before. While I could no longer sustain the demanding schedule of working as a business owner 15 hours a day, 7 days a week, I remained determined to pursue my passion for technology. My decision to embark on this new journey as a data engineer stemmed from three primary motivations. First and foremost, it allowed me to engage in work that I truly love. Additionally, it provided an opportunity to strike a balance between my professional life and personal well-being. Lastly, it offered a means to deepen my understanding of my condition through data science. With these driving forces, I set forth on this path, eager to embrace the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

  • May 2023

    Ready to Roll

    As I commemorate the one-year milestone since the life-altering trauma, I am thrilled to announce my successful completion of the data engineer program, officially marking my entry into the realm of professional software development. While I have been coding since high school, I have never experienced the level of excitement and anticipation that I feel now. Equipped with the knowledge and skills acquired throughout my journey, I am fully prepared to embark on this new chapter of my life, dedicated to producing high-quality code and creating meaningful technological solutions.

  • June 2023

    Project Started

    With a complete arsenal of skills necessary to undertake this ambitious project, there is no justification for further delay. Setting my sights on turning this vision into reality, I initiated the endeavor on my very birthday, coinciding with the release of my 100th GitHub project. These numbers carry profound significance and affirm my belief in the tremendous potential of this venture. As I reflect upon this moment, a single word encapsulates my sentiments: SATISFACTION.
